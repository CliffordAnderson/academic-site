abstract: "The first all-encompassing bibliography of the works of Abraham Kuyper (1837–1920)—Dutch theologian, journalist, and politician, and above all prolific writer—and the first in English. The fruit of Tjitze Kuipers's lifelong research, it documents publications ranging from daily newspaper editorials and pamphlets to a thousand-page political program and academic works such as an encyclopedia of theology, organized chronologically with bibliographical data and notes on contents and context for each item. Translated from the Dutch by Clifford B. Anderson and Dagmare Houniet."
